# CrusaderBot — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-05-27_

CrusaderBot ("the Service") is a Telegram-first, multi-user automated
trading assistant operating on Polymarket prediction markets. This policy
explains what data the Service processes, why, and how it is protected.

The Service currently operates in **paper-trading mode** — no real capital is
deployed. Sections describing on-chain funds and live execution apply only if
and when live trading is explicitly enabled by the operator.

## Who we are

- Service: CrusaderBot (part of WalkerMind OS)
- Operator / Data Controller: Bayue Walker
- Contact: bayuewalker88@gmail.com

## Data we process

- **Telegram account data**: your Telegram user ID, username, and language
  setting, as provided by Telegram when you interact with the bot. Used to
  identify your account and route messages.
- **Trading configuration**: risk preset, strategy selection, category
  filters, copy-trade targets, and notification preferences you set.
- **Account activity**: paper balances, positions, orders, fills, ledger
  entries, and portfolio snapshots generated by your use of the Service.
- **Wallet / deposit data** (live mode only): deposit addresses and on-chain
  transaction references associated with your account.
- **Operational logs**: structured logs and error reports (e.g. Sentry) used
  to diagnose faults. These may contain your Telegram user ID and request
  metadata, but never secrets.

We do **not** collect real names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment
card data through the bot.

## How we use data

- To operate the Service: execute paper (and, if enabled, live) trading
  workflows, monitor positions, and deliver notifications.
- To enforce safety controls: risk gates, deduplication, and the kill switch.
- To diagnose and fix faults via logs and error monitoring.

We do **not** sell your data or use it for advertising.

## Legal basis

We process the data above on the basis of performing the service you request
and our legitimate interest in operating it safely and reliably.

## Storage and security

- Data is stored in managed PostgreSQL (Supabase) with Row Level Security
  enabled, plus Redis and time-series storage for operational metrics.
- Secrets and credentials are held in environment configuration only and are
  never stored in the repository or exposed to users.
- Access to operational controls is restricted and audited.

## Third parties

The Service integrates with: Telegram (messaging), Polymarket / Polygon
(market data and, in live mode, execution and settlement), Supabase
(database), and error-monitoring providers. Data shared with each is limited
to what is required for that function. Each third party operates under its own
privacy policy.

## Data retention

Operational and trading records are retained while your account is active and
for a reasonable period afterward for accounting, audit, and dispute
resolution. Logs are retained per the monitoring provider's default window.
