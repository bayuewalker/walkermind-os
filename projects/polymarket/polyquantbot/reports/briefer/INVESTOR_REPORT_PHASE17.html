Known Limitations & Risks

Alpha Model Not Yet Optimized
The signal engine is in validation mode. Edge is not consistently above the 2% threshold required for profitable deployment. Win rate and net PnL are not yet representative of the intended live strategy.
No Real Capital Deployed
All current execution is paper trading. Live mode requires explicit activation via ENABLE_LIVE_TRADING=true and TRADING_MODE=LIVE guards. This has not been activated.
Market Resolution PnL Pending
Post-settlement PnL updates (when Polymarket resolves a market) are not yet wired. Realized PnL figures will remain incomplete until this is implemented.
Intelligence Layer Partial
The Bayesian updater and drift detection modules are built but not yet fully integrated into execution decisions. Signals are primarily EV-based at this stage.
Market Condition Sensitivity
Prediction market liquidity can thin significantly around major events. The system's liquidity filter is designed to handle this, but performance may vary in low-liquidity market conditions.
Drawdown Metric (Dashboard)
The /performance Telegram command currently returns drawdown = 0.0. The live drawdown circuit-breaker is enforced correctly; the display metric requires an equity curve calculation to be added.
